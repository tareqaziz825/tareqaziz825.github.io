About

Turning data into
intelligent systems.

I am a Computer Science graduate from BRAC University, Dhaka, with a deep research-driven focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. My work centers on building intelligent, data-driven systems — particularly in video understanding, anomaly detection, and applied ML.

My undergraduate thesis introduced a novel dual-stream framework for context-aware zero-shot anomaly detection in surveillance videos. Combining TimeSformer, DPC-RNN, and CLIP, the system detects unseen anomalous events without any labeled anomaly data, achieving 84.5% ROC-AUC on UCF-Crime — outperforming AnomalyCLIP.

Beyond research, I have built applied ML pipelines for spectrophotometric analysis, uncertainty-aware clustering, and financial prediction — honing skills in data preprocessing, feature engineering, and rigorous experimental evaluation.

I am actively seeking entry-level ML engineering, data science, or research assistant roles where I can contribute meaningfully and grow at the intersection of research and engineering.
